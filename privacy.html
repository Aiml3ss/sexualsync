Privacy Policy

Private by default, explicit about the data.

Sexualsync is a private room for adult partners. This policy explains what we collect, what stays on your device, who helps us run the service, and how export and deletion work.

Effective May 26, 2026

What we collect

  • Account details, such as email address, display name, workspace membership, invite status, and profile settings you choose to enter.
  • Room content, such as asks, approved acts, shared fantasies, limits, comments, Shelf links, saved passages, reactions, status history, and encrypted Vault media that you choose to upload.
  • Device-only content, such as private notes stored in your browser. These notes are not sent to our API unless you choose to turn one into an Ask, share it to Inspiration, or include it in an export prepared on that device.
  • Product feedback, such as feedback messages, sentiment, contact preference, and the page or surface where feedback was submitted.
  • App settings, such as notification preferences, install state, privacy mode, local app-lock preference, and workspace deletion status.
  • Operational records, such as audit events, timestamps, request metadata, rate-limit records, error diagnostics, and security logs. We design audit logs to store action metadata, not explicit room content.
  • Push notification subscription data if you enable push notifications.

How we use it

  • To create and protect your private room.
  • To show each partner the right asks, ideas, limits, Shelf items, reactions, and notifications.
  • To keep workspace membership and ownership boundaries enforced.
  • To send invite, request, deletion, and notification emails when email is configured.
  • To review product feedback, respond when you ask us to follow up, and improve the service.
  • To prevent abuse, debug errors, rate-limit expensive actions, and keep the service reliable.

AI-assisted features

Some features can refine wording, suggest prompts, decorate labels, or generate short room copy. When you use those features, the text needed for that request may be sent to the configured LLM provider. Do not put anything into AI-assisted fields that you do not want processed for that feature.

Sexualsync does not generate explicit images or videos.

Vault media is not sent to AI-assisted features.

Private Vault media

If enabled, Private Vault lets active workspace members upload intimate clips, screenshots, encrypted titles, encrypted comments, and encrypted moment notes for that workspace. The app encrypts Vault media and Vault text in the browser before upload using the passphrase you enter. Sexualsync stores encrypted bytes and metadata needed to deliver the feature, such as workspace membership, timestamps, file size, reactions, and audit events.

What encryption protects: the contents of Vault media and text are unreadable to Sexualsync, to its infrastructure providers, and to anyone with database access. The passphrase is never sent to the server.

What encryption does not protect: once Vault media is decrypted in your browser for playback, your device can capture it. Anyone with physical access to an unlocked device can take a screen recording, screenshot, or photo of the screen, and operating-system features like AirPlay, screen sharing, or accessibility tools can mirror what is on screen. The in-app player disables a few download affordances as a hint, not as a guarantee. Treat anything you put in Vault as content you trust on the devices you and your partner use.

Do not upload non-consensual intimate media, content involving minors, illegal content, or media you do not have the right to share. Deleting a workspace is intended to remove Vault metadata and encrypted media from active app storage.

Service providers

We use infrastructure and service providers to run Sexualsync, including Cloudflare for hosting, edge storage, and encrypted Vault object storage, Supabase for authentication and future app data, Resend for email delivery when enabled, RedGifs only when you save or play RedGifs links, and optional diagnostics services if configured.

These providers may process data as needed to deliver their services, secure the platform, or comply with their own legal obligations.

Deletion and export

You can download a JSON export from the app's Account and data area or from Privacy's "See for yourself" controls. The export is prepared on your device and may include local private notes from that browser.

Workspace owners can schedule deletion inside Settings. The app uses a seven-day grace period and emails both partners when closure is scheduled. After final deletion, the room is intended to be permanently removed from active app storage.

You can also request account-data deletion or help with an export by emailing hello@sexualsync.io. We may need enough information to verify that you control the account or workspace.

We do not promise recovery after deletion. Operational providers may retain logs for limited periods under their own retention and security practices.

Retention, sale, and ads

We keep account, room, Vault metadata, support, safety, and operational records only as long as reasonably needed to provide the service, protect users, debug reliability, honor deletion/export requests, resolve disputes, or meet legal obligations.

Sexualsync does not sell personal data. We do not build ad profiles from room content, Vault metadata, private notes, Shelf saves, or intimate activity records.

If advertising, analytics, or billing features are added later, this policy should be updated before those features process user data.

Security

We use login-gated access, workspace membership checks, content security headers, token hashing for review links, rate limits on AI-backed endpoints, and private-by-default product design. No system is perfect, especially with sensitive content, so use Sexualsync only with someone you trust.

Age and consent

Sexualsync is only for adults 18+ using it with consenting adult partners. Do not use the app for minors, coercion, harassment, non-consensual intimate imagery, sexual exploitation, or illegal content.
